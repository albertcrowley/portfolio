## Bio
Al Crowley is a Principal Software Engineer at [TCG](https://tcg.com), a certified B Corporation focused on modernizing IT solutions for the U.S. Federal Government. With over three decades in software engineering, Al splits his time between full-stack client delivery and serving as a DevSecOps Capability Captain.

He holds deep expertise in a variety of tech stacks, including Java, PHP, JavaScript, Python, HTML, and CSS. Al is a fervent advocate for DevOps principles, continually seeking opportunities to mature CI/CD pipelines, implement Infrastructure as Code, and enhance monitoring.

Al's work at TCG emphasizes open solutions that deliver long-term value, transparency, and positive social impact. He holds degrees from Villanova (Computer Science and Honors Comprehensive Science) and a Master's in Computer Science from the University of Pennsylvania.
